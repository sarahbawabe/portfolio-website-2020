DESIGN


STICKER DESIGN

Created and designed stickers for the Brown University Physics WiSE (Women in Science & Engineering) and for the Brown Elementary After-School Mentoring (BEAM) program. All stickers were hand-drawn and/or hand-illustrated using Adobe Illustrator.

POSTER DESIGN

I design posters for the Brown University Physics WiSE (Women in Science & Engineering), Delta Gamma, and other organizations when needed. Some posters were also designed as part of art projects, or simply in my free time. All posters were made using Adobe Illustrator.

LOGO DESIGN

Created and designed logos for Unlimited Basement Finishing Systems, Delta Gamma at Brown, the Norwell Cultural Council (together with artist Nathan Nottingham) and more. All logos were hand-illustrated using Adobe Illustrator.

SPRING WEEKEND
T-SHIRT DESIGN

Won the Brown University Spring Weekend T-Shirt Contest in 2018. The design was inspired by the iconic sculpture "Blueno" currently on the Brown campus. You can read more about Blueno here.
