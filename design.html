DESIGN


STICKER DESIGN

Created and designed stickers for the Brown University Physics WiSE (Women in Science & Engineering) and for the Brown Elementary After-School Mentoring (BEAM) program. All stickers were hand-drawn and/or hand-illustrated using Adobe Illustrator.

POSTER DESIGN

I design posters for the Brown University Physics WiSE (Women in Science & Engineering), Delta Gamma, and other organizations when needed. I also designed the graduation pamphlet for my high school graduation in 2017. All posters are made using Adobe Illustrator.

LOGO DESIGN

Created and designed logos for the Norwell Cultural Council (together with artist Nathan Nottingham) and for the Brown University Physics WiSE (Women in Science & Engineering). Both projects required following specific guidelines, which provided an additional, but interesting, challenge. All logos were hand-illustrated using Adobe Illustrator.
